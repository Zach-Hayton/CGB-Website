Recruiting Process

Application Timeline

Our recruitment process for Fall 2026 is outlined below.

1

Info Sessions

Attend an info session to learn about CGB, meet current members, and ask questions. Check Baylor Connect for dates and RSVP.

2

Coffee Chats

Schedule informal conversations with current members to learn more about the organization and get your questions answered before applying.

3

Applications Open

Submit your application including resume and short-answer questions. Applications typically open at the start of each semester.

4

Interviews

Selected applicants complete a case interview to assess problem-solving skills. Don't worry – we provide resources to help you prepare!

5

Offers Extended

Successful candidates receive offers to join CGB. New member orientation and training begins shortly after.
