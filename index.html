About CGB

Bridging Classroom Knowledge with Real-World Impact

The Consulting Group at Baylor is a premier student-led consulting organization that provides hands-on consulting experience while delivering valuable solutions to organizations across Texas.

Our members gain practical experience in strategic analysis, problem-solving, and client management while developing the professional skills needed to excel in consulting, finance, and business leadership roles.
